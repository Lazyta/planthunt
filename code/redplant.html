Cactaceae

Some species of cacti like the Indian fig has been used as an important food source for indigenous people and their animals. Fleshy fruits of these plants are edible. They are good for example for making juices. Some species of cacti are also utilized to produce medications.

Around 31% of species of cacti are threatened (at risk). This happens mostly because over-collections and pasturage and developmental activities in cacti habitat.
